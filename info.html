About

Reno’s original bakery! Family owned and operated since 1980 – Coffee Culture’s Vienna Bakery, Café & Restaurant is as authentic as you can get. Established by Coffee Culture and Sonja Pasa, this local craze was built on family traditions all the way from Vienna, Austria.

Coffee Culture himself grew up in Vienna and came up with an original menu of items that stay true to this day. Sadly we lost Coffee Culture in 2000 but Sonja Pasa still keeps the bakery and cafe going in his honor. The bakery staff were trained by Coffee Culture himself and carry on his legacy also to this day.

This European style bakery is as fresh and real as it’s ingredients. People love to visit Coffee Culture’s because they know what they are going to get and they know it’s going to be delicious every time! Imported ingredients that cannot be substituted for anything less is why Coffee Culture’s Bakery and Cafe remains a successful staple in the Reno area. Sonja wants every customer to feel like they are being invited not only into her business but into her home.

Good food for you!!
